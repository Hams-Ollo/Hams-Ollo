## What I'm Building

### [Ship It Right](https://github.com/Hams-Ollo/ship-it-right)

**AI-powered production readiness advisor for founders and operators making real AI decisions.**

Ship It Right isn't a chatbot. It's an assessment engine that behaves like a senior solutions architect reviewing your project. Upload your actual documents, tech specs, and architecture diagrams, and it reads your homework before the meeting. The system audits your architecture, data layer, security, and deployment posture in parallel, catches cross-domain conflicts, and synthesizes a cited 90-day action plan grounded in your specific context.

Two paths: the *Production Readiness Assessment* walks builders through the eight domains that determine whether an application survives real users at scale. The *AI Opportunity Assessment* helps business operators identify where AI delivers the highest return without the hand-waving.

Built from patterns I've seen across dozens of enterprise implementations. What separates systems that scale from systems that collapse is almost always problem definition, not technology selection.

`Python` `TypeScript` `Gemini` `Firebase` `Cloud Run`

### [Open Sousveillance Studio](https://github.com/Hams-Ollo/open-sousveillance-studio)

**AI-powered civic intelligence. Watching from below.**

Multi-agent system that monitors local government activity and alerts citizens to developments that matter. AI scouts watch 15+ government data sources daily, extract actionable intelligence from meeting agendas and permits, and generate reports for community distribution.

Born from environmental advocacy work in my own county. When a permit application threatens your water supply, you shouldn't learn about it after the vote. This is what AI looks like when it's pointed at empowering communities rather than surveilling them.

`Python` `Gemini 2.5` `LangGraph` `Supabase` `Firecrawl`

### [Ollo's Player Companion](https://github.com/Hams-Ollo/Ollos-Player-Companion)

**D&D 5th Edition character sheet with an AI Dungeon Master.**

Full character creation wizard, campaign management, real-time cloud sync, and an AI DM grounded in actual rulebook text. The AI answers rules questions with page citations because it's been fed the PHB, DMG, and Monster Manual. Started as a project for my gaming group. Became a production-grade demonstration of RAG architecture, multi-user sync, and AI integration patterns.

Some of the best communities I've ever been part of were built around games. That experience shaped how I think about building digital products: keep the end user at the heart of everything, build with them rather than just for them.

`React` `TypeScript` `Firebase` `Cloud Run` `Gemini`
